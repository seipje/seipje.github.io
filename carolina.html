Carolina Marini

Character Designer:
Media Informatics and New Media Art Student of NKU.
I was born in Argentina but raised in Kentucky for most of my years. I am 23 years old and I love drawing to my hearts content. I have been drawing since I can remember but it's not easy once you start taking in responsibilities. When I draw, I feel the emotions and personalities of the character, which I think is the best way to be able to draw and bring them to life. I also draw animals, robots, hybrids of monsters and humans, armor and anything else you can think of. On my free time I play videos games, watch a bunch of independent films and movies, I cook when I feel like it, and I am in love with kayaking.
